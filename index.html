Topic Modeling

Natural Language Processing

Analyzed a dataset of 2000+ newspaper articles and the topic each newspaper was about. Performed Topic Modeling method using Python to extract certain topics based on the newspaper texts only. I cleaned the data in 3 ways; TF-IDF Normal Cleaning, TF-IDF Frequency Filter, TF-IDF Nouns and Noun Phrases only. After that I used LSI and LDA Models to extract 5 most useful words from the text and finally used Coherence scoring method to determine which cleaning method and model were best.
